About the Band

It is an American hard rock band formed in Hollywood, Los Angeles, in the Sunset Strip area in 1985. It was founded by vocalist and pianist Axl Rose, and guitarist Izzy Stradlin. It is one of the most successful rock bands of all time, having sold more than a hundred million records, is considered a global icon of music and is part of the prestigious Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
